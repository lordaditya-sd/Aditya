About Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya

Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya (DSVV) is a unique institution focused on blending modern scientific education with the deep-rooted spiritual and cultural heritage of India. Located in the holy town of Haridwar, the university emphasizes value-based learning, yoga, Indian culture, self-development, and social service.

सम्मानित प्रवक्ता - डॉ. अरूणेश पाराशर

हरिद्वार देव संस्कृति विश्वविद्यालय के पर्यटन विभाग के डॉ. अरूणेश पाराशर को आई.एच.एम. व ग्राफिक एरा विश्वविद्यालय देहरादून ने सम्मानित किया।

डॉ. पाराशर ने आई.एच.एम. में टेक्नोलॉजी के साथ एथिक्स नियमों पर अपना प्रस्तुतिकरण किया था।

इसके साथ ही ग्राफिक एरा के 7 दिवसीय फैकल्टी डवलपमेंट कार्यक्रम में शिक्षकों को रिसर्च फिलॉसफी पर अपना व्याख्यान दिया।

MoU with Vytautas Magnus University, Lithuania

In a landmark development for global academic exchange, Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya (DSVV), Haridwar, India, and Vytautas Magnus University (VMU), Kaunas, Lithuania, have entered into a formal partnership through the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU).

This strategic collaboration aims to promote mutual academic and cultural exchange, creating a dynamic platform for knowledge-sharing, research cooperation, and global educational outreach.

MoU with Quantum University, Roorkee

In a remarkable step towards strengthening academic and research collaboration, Quantum University, Roorkee, and Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya (DSVV), Haridwar, have officially signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU).

This partnership, formalized on 26th March 2025, signifies a shared commitment to fostering mutual cooperation in the fields of Humanities, Tourism & Management Studies, Media Studies, and Health.

MoU with Line Monk Pty Ltd, Australia

Haridwar, March 4, 2025: Dev Sanskriti Vishwavidyalaya (DSVV), Haridwar, has entered into a significant partnership with Line Monk Pty Ltd, a leading Australian technology company, by signing a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU).

This collaboration aims to foster innovation, research, and the development of ethical technology solutions, particularly in the fields of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning.
